MediaX — Dedup Engine

Content-aware deduplication with cryptographic and perceptual hashing.

Context

Large media libraries often contain exact and near-duplicates spread across devices.

Problem

Need to detect exact duplicates and perceptual similarity (frames, crops, partial clips).

Solution

Combined hashing approach, batch triage flows, quarantines, and detailed reports.

Results

Significant storage reclaimed with transparent, repeatable actions.

Tech

Rust, SIMD, CLI, reporting pipeline.

Impact

Reclaimed 30% storage in large datasets.
